Why Visit Bangkok?

Bangkok is a city of contrasts — gleaming temples sit beside neon-lit night markets, and Michelin-starred restaurants share streets with legendary $1 noodle stalls. Whether you're here for three days or three weeks, Bangkok rewards every type of traveller with an experience that's impossible to replicate anywhere else in the world.

The city is one of Southeast Asia's best-value destinations, with world-class hotels at a fraction of European prices and street food that rivals any fine dining experience. Getting here is easy with direct flights from most major cities, and the BTS Skytrain makes getting around a breeze.
