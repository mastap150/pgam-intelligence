## YMYL COMPLIANCE NOTE
Health content falls under Google's "Your Money or Your Life" (YMYL) category — the highest scrutiny tier for search ranking.

**Mandatory for every article:**
- Named medical reviewer with credentials (MD, RD, PhD)
- Publication and last-reviewed date visible
- Citations to PubMed/peer-reviewed sources
- Medical disclaimer in footer
- No absolute medical claims ("this cures/treats X")
