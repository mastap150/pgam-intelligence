## CATEGORY DEFINITIONS & DESCRIPTIONS

### 1. Nutrition
**Category H1:** Nutrition Science for Real People — Not Diet Culture
**Description:** Evidence-based nutrition guidance covering macronutrients, micronutrients, gut health, meal planning, and dietary strategies backed by peer-reviewed research. We strip out the fads and tell you what the science actually says.
**Subcategories:** Macronutrients · Vitamins & Minerals · Gut Health · Meal Planning · Diets & Protocols · Anti-Inflammatory Eating · Blood Sugar · Weight Management
**Article count goal:** 200+

### 2. Fitness
**Category H1:** Train Smarter. Recover Better. Perform Longer.
**Description:** Science-backed fitness content covering strength training, cardiovascular health, recovery, mobility, and sport-specific performance. Every protocol explained with the physiological rationale.
**Subcategories:** Strength Training · Cardio & VO2 Max · HIIT · Zone 2 · Recovery · Mobility · Injury Prevention · Wearables & Data
**Article count goal:** 180+

### 3. Mental Health
**Category H1:** Mental Health, Backed by Neuroscience
**Description:** Evidence-based guides to stress, anxiety, sleep, mindfulness, burnout, and emotional wellbeing. Written with clinical accuracy and reviewed by mental health professionals. No toxic positivity — just tools that work.
**Subcategories:** Stress & Anxiety · Sleep & Mental Health · Depression Awareness · Mindfulness · Burnout · Therapy Resources · Breathwork · Neuroscience Basics
**Article count goal:** 140+

### 4. Longevity
**Category H1:** The Science of Living Longer — and Better
**Description:** Cutting-edge research on aging, lifespan extension, cellular health, biomarkers, and lifestyle interventions that move the needle. Covering Blue Zones, VO2 max, muscle preservation, and the latest in longevity medicine.
**Subcategories:** Aging Science · Blue Zones · Biomarkers · Fasting · Supplements · Cold & Heat Therapy · Hormones · Epigenetics
**Article count goal:** 90+

### 5. Sleep
**Category H1:** Sleep Is Your Most Important Health Variable
**Description:** Comprehensive sleep science covering sleep architecture, circadian rhythm, sleep disorders, and evidence-based protocols for sleep optimization. Featuring research from leading sleep scientists.
**Subcategories:** Sleep Science · Circadian Rhythm · Sleep Disorders · Sleep Hygiene · Supplements · Sleep & Performance · Tools & Trackers
**Article count goal:** 75+

### 6. Conditions
**Category H1:** Understand Your Health Conditions — In Plain English
**Description:** Condition-specific guides written by medical professionals covering causes, mechanisms, evidence-based treatment approaches, and lifestyle interventions for common and complex health conditions.
**Subcategories:** Metabolic Health · Heart & Cardiovascular · Thyroid Health · Hormonal Health · Autoimmune · Digestive Disorders · Mental Health Conditions
**Article count goal:** 120+
