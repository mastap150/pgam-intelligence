## UNIVERSAL FORMATTING RULES (Both Sites)

### SEO Formatting
- **H1:** One per article, includes exact-match target keyword
- **H2s:** 4–8 per article. Include secondary keywords naturally. No keyword stuffing.
- **H3s:** Use within long H2 sections. Max 3 levels deep (H1 → H2 → H3).
- **Bold:** Use for key terms, key stats, and important warnings. Max 1–2 bolds per paragraph.
- **Internal links:** 3–5 per article. Contextual placement, descriptive anchor text.
- **Images:** Every 500 words minimum. Descriptive alt text with keyword where natural.
- **Lists:** Use for steps, comparisons, and enumerations of 3+ items.
- **Tables:** Use for comparisons, data, budgets, timelines.

### CTA Placement Rules
| Position | CTA Type |
|----------|----------|
| After intro (≈ 200 words in) | Inline contextual link |
| Middle of article (50% mark) | Newsletter inline CTA or related article link |
| End of article | Newsletter subscription block |
| Sidebar (desktop) | Newsletter or category browse CTA |
| Bottom of page | Related articles grid (3 cards) |
