## Ad Network Tiers

### Tier 1 — Premium Programmatic (Target: 50K+ sessions/month)
- **Mediavine** (min 50,000 sessions/month) — Travel RPM: $18–$40, Health RPM: $25–$55

### Tier 2 — Mid-Tier (Current scale)
- **Ezoic** (no minimum, AI-optimized ad placement) — Travel RPM: $10–$22, Health RPM: $15–$30
- Use Ezoic to grow toward Mediavine thresholds

### Tier 3 — While Growing (Under 10K sessions)
- **Google AdSense** — RPM: $4–$12 (travel), $8–$18 (health)
- Low revenue but zero minimum — use as placeholder

**Recommendation:** Apply to Ezoic immediately. Target Mediavine at 50K sessions.

## AD PLACEMENT RULES (Both Sites)

| Rule | Reason |
|------|--------|
| Max 3 in-content ads per article | User experience + Mediavine/AdThrive policy |
| No ad within first 200 words | Google policy on page experience |
| No ad directly above/below another ad | Accidental clicks, policy risk |
| Sidebar sticky ad: disable below 768px | Overlaps content on mobile |
| Anchor bottom mobile ad: delay 5 seconds | Reduces bounce from accidental close |
| No pop-up ads | Core Web Vitals penalty + user experience |

## AFFILIATE CONTENT STRATEGY

### Disclosure (Non-Negotiable)
Every article or section containing affiliate links must include:
```
*This article contains affiliate links. If you make a purchase through these links, 
we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This does not influence our 
editorial recommendations — our content is editorially independent.*
```

### Native Affiliate Formats (Higher CTR than Banner Links)
1. **Comparison tables** with affiliate CTAs per row → "Check price" or "Book now" buttons
2. **Callout boxes** within articles: "Where we book hotels for this region: [Booking.com link]"
3. **"Our Pick" product cards** with image, key specs, price, and CTA
4. **Bottom-of-article resource section:** "Tools we use and recommend"

## PAGE LAYOUT OPTIMIZATION FOR REVENUE

### Core Principle
Revenue-per-visitor = f(ad density × viewability × time on page × affiliate placement)

### Layout Decisions That Move Revenue
| Change | Expected RPM Impact |
|--------|-------------------|
| Add sticky sidebar ad (desktop) | +15–25% |
| Add anchor bottom banner (mobile) | +10–20% |
| Move first in-content ad above fold on mobile | +8–15% |
| Add comparison table with affiliate CTAs | +affiliate revenue |
| Improve page speed (Core Web Vitals) | +10–30% organic traffic (not RPM, but total revenue) |
| Increase average time on page with better content | +10–20% viewable impressions |

### What NOT to Do (Revenue-Destroys)
- Pop-ups or pop-unders → Google penalty + user bounce
- Auto-playing video ads → Mediavine will not approve
- More than 3 in-content ads → diminishing returns, policy risk
- Interstitial ads → Core Web Vitals failure
- Affiliate links without disclosure → FTC violation + Google trust signal damage

## SPONSORED CONTENT FRAMEWORK

### Destination.com Sponsored Post Guidelines
- Minimum 1,500 words
- Must meet same editorial quality standards as organic content
- Labeled: "Sponsored" or "Partnered with [Brand]" — above the fold, clearly visible
- No follow links only (Google policy for paid placement)
- Rate card: $800–$3,000 depending on placement and traffic
- Categories: hotels, airlines, tourism boards, travel gear brands

### HealthNation.com Sponsored Post Guidelines
- Stricter than destination.com — sponsored health content must not contradict editorial science standards
- Medical reviewer still required before publication
- "Sponsored" label mandatory — no exceptions
- Rate card: $600–$2,500
- Categories: supplement brands (evidence-based only), health apps, fitness platforms, insurance
- Hard no: MLM companies, unproven treatments, anything contradicted by site's own content
