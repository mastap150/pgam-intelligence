## What every PGAM campaign includes

- Managed media buying across our SpringServe/ClearLine demand stack (premium CTV + curated OLV supply, brand-safe by default).
- **PGAM Attention** — per-impression attention score (engagement × completion × viewability composite), proprietary to PGAM.
- **PGAM Attribution** — TFN-matched call attribution down to creative × DMA × daypart (Invoca read-only key required per advertiser).
- Creative QA, traffic, and pacing handled by PGAM AdOps.
- Weekly written optimization recap. Mid-flight creative + audience adjustments included.
- Real-time dashboard access (delivery, pacing, attention, attribution).
- Quarterly business review with attribution narrative + lift readout.
